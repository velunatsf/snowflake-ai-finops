The Three FinOps Habits for AI

Habit 1: ESTIMATE

"Test on 10 rows. Project to full dataset. Decide."

Never run AI at scale without first testing on a small sample, measuring the cost, and projecting to your full dataset size.

Habit 2: MONITOR

"The dashboard is your early warning system."

The dashboard you built today should be checked weekly. Set alert thresholds before problems become emergencies.

Habit 3: CHOOSE

"The cheapest model that meets your quality bar."

Model selection is a cost decision. Upgrade only when the output genuinely requires higher capability.

C

Why Cortex AI Rewrites the FinOps Rules

Traditional FinOps focuses on warehouse compute. But AI workloads break four fundamental assumptions. These principles (covered in depth in Module 07) explain why dedicated AI monitoring is essential.

01 - Credit Burn

AI credit consumption scales faster than traditional compute. Thousands of COMPLETE/SUMMARIZE/EXTRACT calls burn credits silently - no warehouse to suspend.

02 - Behavior

AI workloads are experimental and hard to predict. Teams switch models frequently and run exploratory prompts at scale. Usage patterns shift weekly.

03 - Visibility

Serverless AI spend is invisible to warehouse dashboards. Cortex AI runs outside warehouses - traditional monitoring misses it entirely.

04 - Signals

Token counts alone don't tell the full story. The same prompt on a large model costs 10x more than on a lighter one. Without tracking model choice, costs stay hidden.

AI Spend Will Grow - Govern It Now

AI token consumption is the fastest-growing line item in cloud data platform bills in 2025. The teams who govern it now will be the ones who can afford to scale it later.

AI Cost Tracking - Quick Reference (7 Views)

Resource Monitors Do NOT Cover AI/Serverless

Resource Monitors cap warehouse compute credits only. AI token credits are billed separately. Use Snowflake Budgets and the ACCOUNT_USAGE views below to track AI spend.

ACCOUNT_USAGE View Tracks Key Columns
CORTEX_AISQL_USAGE_HISTORY Analyst, Agents, Intelligence TOKEN_CREDITS, TOKENS, QUERY_TAG
CORTEX_AI_FUNCTIONS_USAGE_HISTORY AI_COMPLETE, SENTIMENT, etc. CREDITS, FUNCTION_NAME, MODEL_NAME
CORTEX_SEARCH_DAILY_USAGE_HISTORY Search indexing & queries CREDITS, SERVICE_NAME, CONSUMPTION_TYPE
DOCUMENT_AI_USAGE_HISTORY Document AI extraction CREDITS_USED, MODEL_NAME
METERING_DAILY_HISTORY Aggregated daily metering CREDITS_BILLED, SERVICE_TYPE
CORTEX_CODE_CLI_USAGE_HISTORY Snowflake CoCo CLI CREDITS, MODEL_NAME, USER_ID
CORTEX_CODE_SNOWSIGHT_USAGE_HISTORY Cortex Code Snowsight CREDITS, MODEL_NAME, USER_ID

All views have ~45 min latency. Budget refresh: 6.5 hr default, 1 hr low-latency. See Module 06 for full queries.
